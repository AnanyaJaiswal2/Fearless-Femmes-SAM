# 🚀 Fearless Femmes

An AI-powered platform empowering women with safety, career growth, emotional wellbeing, and community support — all in one ecosystem.

---

## 🧭 Overview

Fearless Femmes is designed to solve real-world challenges faced by women:

- 🚫 Lack of career guidance  
- ⚠️ Safety uncertainty  
- 💬 Emotional & health awareness gaps  

It focuses on **preventive intelligence**, not just reactive alerts.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🛡️ Safety System
- One-tap SOS alert  
- Real-time location tracking  
- AI-based risk scoring  
- Safe route suggestions  
- Emergency contact alerts  

### 📞 Fake Call Generator (AI-Powered)
- Generates realistic fake calls in unsafe situations  
- Powered by Gemini API  
- Customizable caller & tone  
- Includes voice + ringtone  

### 💼 Career Growth
- AI career roadmap  
- Skill-gap detection  
- Resume builder  
- Mentor connections  

### ❤️ Emotional & Health
- Mood tracking  
- Cycle tracking  
- AI insights & recommendations  

### 🌐 Community
- Anonymous discussions  
- Support groups  
- Mentorship & events  

---

## 🧠 How It Works

1. User logs in  
2. Selects module (Safety / Career / Health / Community)  
3. AI processes data (risk, career, mood)  
4. Firebase handles storage & alerts
